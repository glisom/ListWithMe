## Description

ListWithMe is the easiest way to create and share shopping lists with anyone in your iMessage conversations. No accounts, no sign-ups—just open a conversation, create a list, and start collaborating.

**Perfect for:**
- Grocery shopping with your partner
- Planning errands with family
- Coordinating party supplies with friends
- Any shared list you need to tackle together

**Powerful Features:**

Smart Input
Type "3 apples" or "milk x2" and ListWithMe automatically sets the quantity for you. No extra taps required.

Stay Organized
Assign categories to group items by aisle. Set priority levels for must-have items. Add due dates for time-sensitive errands.

Real-Time Collaboration
Everyone in the conversation sees updates instantly. Check off items as you shop and watch the list update for everyone.

Activity Feed
See who added, completed, or removed items. Know exactly what changed and when.

Quick Actions
Swipe to complete or delete items. Use batch selection to handle multiple items at once. Clear all completed items with one tap.

Find Items Fast
Search through long lists instantly. Sort by name, category, priority, or due date.

ListWithMe lives entirely in iMessage—no separate app cluttering your home screen. Just pure, simple list sharing with the people who matter most.
